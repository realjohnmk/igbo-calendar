# Ọdụ Oge Igbo — Igbo Calendar

A modern Progressive Web App (PWA) displaying the traditional Igbo calendar with rotating market days (Eke, Orie, Afọ, Nkwo). Installable on Android and works fully offline.

## Features

- **4-day Igbo week** — Eke, Orie, Afọ, and Nkwo, each colour-coded on every calendar day
- **Market day cycle** — anchored to a traditional reference date, accurate for any year
- **Jump to any date** — tap the month name to pick any month and year instantly
- **Today button** — one tap returns you to the current month
- **Installable PWA** — add to Android home screen like a native app
- **Offline support** — works without internet after first visit
- **Dark traditional theme** — deep earth tones with gold accents

---

## The Igbo Week

The traditional Igbo week (*Izu*) has four days, not seven:

| Day | Meaning | Colour |
|-----|---------|--------|
| **Eke** | Day of Creation | 🟠 Clay |
| **Orie** | Day of Rest | 🟢 Forest |
| **Afọ** | Day of Earth | 🟣 Purple |
| **Nkwo** | Day of Trade | 🔵 Blue |

Markets rotate on this 4-day cycle across different towns, creating a vibrant regional trading network.

## Tech Stack

- Vanilla HTML, CSS, JavaScript — no frameworks, no dependencies
- SVG icons — crisp at any size

- Web App Manifest — enables "Add to Home Screen" on Android

---

## Cultural Notes

- The Igbo lunar year (*Ọnwa*) has 13 months, each approximately 28 days (7 Igbo weeks)
- Key festivals such as *Iri Ji* (New Yam Festival) and *Ọmabe* masquerade are timed by this calendar
- The market day cycle is deeply tied to agriculture, trade, and ancestral rites across Igboland
